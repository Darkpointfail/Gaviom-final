Official Rules

These Official Rules govern all Gaviom consumer sweepstakes promotions (each, a "Promotion" or "Sweepstakes"). By entering, you agree to these rules and to any Promotion-specific terms below. If there is a conflict, the Promotion-specific section controls for that Promotion only.

1. Overview

Gaviom operates skill-free prize promotions that comply with the U.S. distinction between illegal lotteries (prize + chance + consideration) and lawful sweepstakes (prize + chance + free alternative method of entry). No purchase or payment is required to enter or win.

  • Each Promotion has a fixed maximum number of entries and published odds at full capacity.
  • All valid paid entries, free mail-in entries, and eligible membership entries for that Promotion are combined into a single random draw pool.
  • Grand Prize drawings for launch Promotions are scheduled for Sunday, July 5, 2026 at approximately 8:00 p.m. Eastern Time, streamed live on YouTube, unless otherwise stated in a Promotion section.
  • Pre-sale entries purchased before the public launch date are held and assigned entry numbers before the applicable drawing, subject to payment confirmation and eligibility verification.
